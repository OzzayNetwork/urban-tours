About Us

Kenya is the heart of East Africa, a vast country that possesses wonderful beaches, massive wilderness areas and a wealth of fascinating tribal rituals.

It is a country full with romance, thrill and adventure – nothing says “classic safari” quite like a Kenyan safari.

It is often seen as the first harbor of call for any African safari or a stepping-stone to subsequent safaris in Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda and Southern Africa. Because of Kenya’s historic tourism development, the country’s infrastructure is refined and its national parks well-maintained with a vast array of accommodation available, to suit every style, taste and budget.

It is home to the Big 5 game animals and with its wide-open plains its habitat is particularly suited to cheetahs. If however, you are looking for a quieter alternative there are other parks that offer a safari holiday that is just as rewarding.

Since Africa is so special, we thought that you require a professional. Africa is our home; we know it confidentially well. We are a boutique safari company specialized in offering well thought and fashioned safari programs. This is to ensure that the best moments of your safari experiences are captured in your memory for a life time.

Urban Adventure Expeditions team is full of passion to ensure that your safari experiences will be captured forever. We help create memories with a peace of mind as you fall in love with Africa.

Our safari consultants are always at hand to ensure that your holiday dreams are reinvented and captured in Africa.

Join us for some of the most unique experiences that Africa does better than anywhere else. We promise to deliver memories to last a lifetime!
